About Us!

Struggle for exellence.

  • FGM Landscaping & Brick Pavers, also known as Freehold Grounds Maintenance, has been creating and maintaining fine landscapes for over 30 years. We pride ourselves on having a mix between positive customer relations & a high level of design skill. Owners of FGM Joe Spicuzza and Scott Kuffner, created this company in 1985 in Freehold, New Jersey. Our office is now located in Millstone New Jersey, after a successful start to the company and the need for a bigger workspace. We are determined to work with our customers in order to ensure they achieve the upmost satisfaction at an affordable price. We are ICPI certified, a licensed pesticide applicator business, and fully insured!
